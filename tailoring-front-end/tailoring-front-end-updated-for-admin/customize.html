How to Take Measurements

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure parallel to the floor.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, keeping the tape measure comfortably loose.

Shoulder

Measure from the edge of one shoulder to the edge of the other shoulder.

Sleeve Length

Measure from the shoulder edge to your desired sleeve length.

Blouse Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to your desired blouse length.

Neck Depth

Measure from the base of the neck to your desired neckline depth.
